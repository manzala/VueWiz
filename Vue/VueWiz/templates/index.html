How It Works

Add Your Resume

Add a normal resume in pdf or text format. Recruiters will use our search to find keywords in your resume or you can give them a link to your resume ("http://vuewiz.com/username")

Add An Intro Video

After recruiters are interested in your resume, they have the chance to watch a 30-45 second video of you introducing yourself and your most interesting project.

Video Interview

Recruiters can quickly set up a video interview with questions made for you. You'll be given a set amount of time to complete the questions.
